# AR.js Implementation - Hiro Marker

## How to Use AR Feature

1. **Open the Cover page** on your mobile device
2. **Click "Try AR Experience"** button
3. **Allow camera permissions** when prompted
4. **Point your camera** at the Hiro marker (see below)
5. **3D model will appear** on the marker!

## Hiro Marker

Download and print the Hiro marker from:
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AR-js-org/AR.js/master/data/images/hiro.png

### Tips for Best Results:

- Print the marker on white paper
- Keep the marker flat and well-lit
- Hold your device steady
- Keep distance about 30-50cm from marker
- Make sure the entire marker is visible in camera

## Alternative: Display on Screen

You can also display the marker on another screen:

1. Open the marker image on a computer/tablet
2. Point your phone camera at the screen
3. The AR experience will work!

## Troubleshooting

**Camera not working?**

- Make sure you're using HTTPS
- Check camera permissions in browser settings
- Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)

**Model not appearing?**

- Ensure good lighting
- Keep marker flat and fully visible
- Try moving closer/further from marker
- Refresh the page and try again

**Performance issues?**

- Close other apps
- Use a newer device if possible
- Ensure stable internet connection

## Technical Details

- **Library**: AR.js with A-Frame
- **Marker Type**: Hiro (preset marker)
- **3D Model**: Same as Cover page (animated cube net)
- **Tracking**: Marker-based AR
- **Browser Support**: Chrome, Safari, Firefox (mobile & desktop)
